Welcome to the CodeComfy Handbook — your reference for generating images and videos with ComfyUI directly inside VS Code.

CodeComfy bridges your editor and your ComfyUI server so you never have to switch windows. Type a prompt in the Command Palette, watch the status bar, and find your finished image or video in the workspace when it is done.

## What you will find here

- **[Getting Started](getting-started/)** — prerequisites, installation, configuration, and your first generation.
- **[Reference](reference/)** — generation limits, architecture overview, troubleshooting, platform notes, and security scope.
